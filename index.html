Digital Publishing

With dwell agency I have worked as the sole programmer on a variety of published advertisements and editorial pieces. This involves embedding Webpage bundles into DPS (Digital Publishing Suite) documents in Adobe Indesign. These pages make up part of a published Magazine which would be available on the Apple Newstand.

Many of these pages used my dps.js library which contains commonly used functions and classes for use in Adobe DPS HTML overlays, in order to make the most of iOS-specific behaviours.

My work in the Digital Publishing sector includes:

  • over 30 front covers for WIRED UK Magazine, from 2013 to 2015
  • A 4-part Interactive advertisement for Graff Diamonds, in British GQ magazine
  • Interactive advertisements for Bentley, Shell, Seiko and Woolmark
  • Animated and Interactive Articles/Covers for GQ, Esquire, and BBC TopGear Magazine
  • Voting etc etc etc
